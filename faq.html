Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MeSnap

What is MeSnap and how does it work?

+

MeSnap is a plastic ID card holder with built-in MagSafe magnets and NFC technology. It allows you to securely attach your university ID card to your iPhone using MagSafe technology. The embedded NFC chip lets you share your contact information when someone taps their phone against it. MeSnap can also be used with a standard lanyard if you prefer.

Which iPhone models is MeSnap compatible with?

+

MeSnap is compatible with all MagSafe-enabled iPhones, which includes the iPhone 12 series and newer. The magnetic attachment is optimized for these models, providing a secure fit.

How do I set up the NFC feature?

+

Once you receive your MeSnap, scan the QR code on the packaging to access our setup page. Follow the simple instructions to program your NFC chip with your contact information. This typically takes less than 2 minutes to complete. After setup, anyone can tap their phone against your MeSnap to instantly receive your contact details.

What ID card sizes does MeSnap accommodate?

+

MeSnap is designed to fit standard credit card sized ID cards (3.37 × 2.125 inches or 85.6 × 54 mm), which is the standard size used by most universities for student ID cards.

Will MeSnap interfere with RFID functionality of my ID card?

+

No, MeSnap is designed with special shielding that prevents interference with the RFID functionality of your ID card. You can still use your ID card for building access, meal plans, and other campus services without removing it from the MeSnap holder.
